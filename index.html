How it works
Local files only · no uploads

This is a Web Audio mixer: two decks feeding a master chain (EQ → FX → Comp → Limiter → Output). Everything runs in your browser. Load songs from your device and mix.

  • Start Audio is required because browsers block audio until user interaction.
  • Record captures the master output and lets you download the file.
  • For best results: keep levels below clipping and use limiter gently.
If you want next upgrades: waveform display, cue points, BPM detect, and exporting WAV.
